Maestro Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 15, 2026

Maestro is a Mental Health app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use
To provide a comprehensive mental health experience, we may collect the following information:

Personal Data: Such as your name and email address when you sign up using Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In.

Health and Activity Data: We use the pedometer and ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION permissions to track physical activity as it relates to your mental wellness.

Audio Data: We use RECORD_AUDIO to enable speech-to-text features and recording functionalities.

2. Location Data
Maestro requests access to your location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) and uses a foreground service for location tracking. This data is used solely to provide location-based features relevant to your mental health journey and is not shared with third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Services
Maestro utilizes several third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

Google Play Services: Used for app functionality and core services.

Firebase (Auth, Messaging, Firestore): Used for user authentication, cloud data storage, and push notifications.

Google Sign-In: To allow easy access to your account.

4. Permissions
By using Maestro, you grant us permission to access:

Internet: To sync your progress with Firebase.

Notifications: To send wellness reminders and alerts (POST_NOTIFICATIONS).

Storage: To save app-related data locally.

Alarms: To schedule exact wellness reminders (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM).

5. Children’s Privacy
Maestro does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it from our servers immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
